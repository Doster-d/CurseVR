\doxysubsection{Подробное описание}
Buffers audio samples and creates Audio\+Clips when the buffer is filled or flushed. 

This class provides a mechanism to collect audio samples over time and create Audio\+Clip instances when specific conditions are met (buffer full or manually flushed). It\textquotesingle{}s primarily used to collect voice data detected by the voice activity detection system for further processing.
